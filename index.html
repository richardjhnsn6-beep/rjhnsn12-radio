About VelvetWave FM

The home of instrumental soft funk and quiet storm radio

Our Mission

VelvetWave FM was founded on the belief that instrumental music speaks directly to the soul. We curate the finest soft funk, quiet storm, and smooth jazz instrumentals from around the world, creating a sanctuary of sound for our listeners 24/7.

No Vocals, Pure Vibes

We specialize exclusively in instrumental tracks. No lyrics, no distractions – just the pure emotional power of music. Our carefully selected playlist features silky basslines, warm keyboards, and grooving drum breaks.

Global Community

Broadcasting to listeners in over 150 countries, VelvetWave FM has become the go-to destination for instrumental soft funk lovers worldwide. Join our community of smooth music enthusiasts.
